Section 4

Email and SMS Communications

Email

By submitting our contact form, you consent to receiving email communications from AI Strategy US related to your inquiry. This may include an initial confirmation, your requested guide, follow-up information about our services, and scheduling assistance. You may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link included in every email we send.

SMS Text Messages

SMS communications are sent only to individuals who have selected a consultation inquiry and explicitly checked the SMS consent box on our contact form. SMS messages are used solely for appointment coordination and follow-up related to your consultation request — not for promotional campaigns.

SMS Program Disclosure

By providing your phone number and checking the SMS consent box, you agree to receive text messages from AI Strategy US, LLC regarding your inquiry and consultation scheduling.

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

Reply STOP at any time to opt out of SMS communications. Reply HELP for assistance. Opting out of SMS does not affect your ability to receive our guide or request a consultation through other means.

SMS consent is not a condition of receiving our services or educational materials.
