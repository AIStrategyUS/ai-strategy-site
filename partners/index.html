About Us

Who We Are

AI Strategy US, LLC is a consulting firm based in Nashville, Tennessee. We work directly with small business owners and operators across the United States to identify where AI can meaningfully improve how they run their business — and to build a clear, practical plan to get there.

Our focus is on practical outcomes, not theoretical strategy. We serve business owners and the managers running key workflows — people who need real solutions, not enterprise-level complexity. We generate consistent inbound demand from SMBs actively seeking AI guidance, and we partner with full-service agencies and implementation teams to ensure those clients get the delivery they need.

Partner Fit

Who This Program Is For

We partner exclusively with full-service AI consulting agencies and implementation teams that can handle a client engagement from start to finish. This program is not designed for single-product vendors, SaaS resellers, or teams that only deliver one piece of an engagement.

  • ✓ Full-service AI consulting agencies with end-to-end capability
  • ✓ Engineering and implementation teams that deliver complete AI projects
  • ✓ Teams experienced in discovery, scoping, build, integration, and ongoing support
  • ✓ Agencies with a track record serving small and mid-size businesses
  • ✓ Teams with current availability and capacity to take on new work

Outside current scope: Healthcare providers, private equity-backed companies, and businesses in heavily regulated industries require specialized compliance knowledge that falls outside our current operating model. We do not refer clients in these categories at this time.

How We Work Together

Three Collaboration Models

Partners can participate in one or all three models depending on their capacity and preference. Commercial terms are negotiated case-by-case based on involvement, client scope, and delivery model.

01

Warm Lead Referrals

Inbound Interest · Handed Off As-Is

A business owner has expressed genuine interest in AI consulting through our intake process. Basic contact verification has been completed. The lead is passed to one partner for follow-up.

  • Verified contact information and source of inquiry
  • No discovery, process mapping, or budget validation completed
  • Delivered to one partner exclusively
  • Partner takes full responsibility for qualification and follow-up
  • Commercial terms: negotiated based on lead volume and arrangement
02

Qualified Opportunities

Discovery Completed · Opportunity Brief Included

We've completed a structured discovery conversation with the prospect and produced a written Opportunity Brief — typically 1 to 2 pages — that gives your team a clear picture of the client before you commit to engagement.

  • Decision-maker path and authority assessment
  • Business process map and current tools/data environment
  • Estimated volumes, pain points, and desired outcomes
  • Budget band, timeline, and key constraints
  • Implementation readiness assessment
  • Recommended handoff approach and suggested first step
  • Partner may accept or decline the opportunity — no obligation
03

Co-Sell Partnership

Active Collaboration · Partner Delivers

We work alongside your team through discovery, scoping, and close. AI Strategy US supports the sales process; the partner agency handles full implementation and ongoing client delivery.

  • Joint discovery and scoping calls with the client
  • Solution direction input and positioning support from our side
  • Active involvement through the close
  • Partner owns implementation, delivery, and ongoing support
  • Retainer and long-term engagement terms addressed upfront
  • Commercial terms negotiated per engagement based on scope and involvement

Working Together

Process Expectations

Lead Exclusivity

Every opportunity is delivered to one partner only. We do not offer the same lead to multiple agencies simultaneously.

Acceptance & Decline

For Qualified Leads, partners may accept or pass on any opportunity. There is no obligation. Declined opportunities may be offered to another partner at our discretion.

Confidentiality

All client information shared through any collaboration model is confidential. Partner agrees not to redistribute, share, or repurpose any shared client data.

Responsiveness

Partners are expected to follow up on delivered opportunities promptly. Warm leads are time-sensitive — delay reduces conversion significantly.

Lead Timing

Opportunity delivery is based on our current pipeline and partner capacity. We do not guarantee volume or fixed delivery frequency. Opportunities are routed as they become available.

Feedback Loop

We ask partners to share outcome updates on delivered opportunities. This helps us calibrate quality, refine scoring, and improve handoff quality over time.
