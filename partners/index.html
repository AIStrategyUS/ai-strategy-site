Partner Fit

We're selective about who we work with

Our clients trust us to connect them with capable, professional teams. That means we only partner with agencies that can handle full-cycle delivery — not single-product vendors or point solutions.

✓  Strong Fit

What we look for in a partner

  • ✓ Full-service AI consulting agencies with end-to-end capability
  • ✓ Engineering and implementation teams that deliver complete AI projects
  • ✓ Teams experienced with discovery, scoping, build, and ongoing support
  • ✓ Experience serving small and mid-size businesses
  • ✓ Strong client communication and responsiveness
  • ✓ Proven delivery track record — not early-stage
  • ✓ Capacity to take on new client work
✗  Not a Fit

What we can't work with

  • ✗ Single-product vendors or SaaS resellers
  • ✗ Agencies that only deliver one narrow tool or platform
  • ✗ Teams without implementation or post-delivery support capability
  • ✗ Healthcare, private equity, or highly regulated industry specialists — outside current scope
  • ✗ Enterprise-only agencies — our clients are small business operators
  • ✗ Agencies without availability or at full capacity

What We Value

The qualities that make a great partner

Beyond delivery capability, the partnerships that work best share a few consistent characteristics.

⚡

Responsive

Business owners move fast when they're motivated. Partners who follow up promptly and communicate clearly close more and waste less time on both sides.

🔧

End-to-End Capable

We need partners who can own the full engagement — discovery, build, integration, and support. Partial capabilities create gaps that hurt the client experience.

🤝

Client-First

Our reputation depends on the experience our shared clients have. Partners who prioritize client outcomes over short-term wins build the kind of relationships that generate referrals.

📋

Process-Oriented

Consistent handoffs, clear communication at each stage, and honest feedback when something isn't the right fit. Good process makes good partnerships.

📈

Proven Track Record

We look for agencies with completed client engagements they can speak to clearly. Early-stage teams without delivery history aren't the right fit at this time.

🔒

Confidentiality

Client information shared as part of any lead or opportunity is treated as confidential. Partners are expected to handle all shared data with professional discretion.

Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

What makes a partner a good fit?

▾
The most important factor is end-to-end delivery capability. We're looking for agencies or teams that can handle a client from discovery through implementation and ongoing support — not teams that only cover one piece of the puzzle. Beyond that, responsiveness and client communication are the qualities that separate good partner relationships from difficult ones.

What industries do your clients come from?

▾
Our inbound clients span a wide range of small business categories — contractors, professional services, real estate, retail, service businesses, and operations-heavy businesses looking to automate manual work. We do not currently serve healthcare providers, private equity-backed companies, or businesses in heavily regulated industries that require specialized compliance knowledge.

Are leads exclusive?

▾
Yes. Each opportunity is delivered to one partner only. We don't distribute the same lead to multiple agencies. That exclusivity is part of what we believe makes this program worth participating in.

What makes a lead "qualified"?

▾
A Qualified Lead means we've completed a discovery conversation and produced a written Opportunity Brief. We assess four primary factors: decision-maker authority, budget strength, urgency, and implementation readiness. Each is scored on a simple rubric, and the brief summarizes what we found so your team can evaluate the opportunity clearly before deciding whether to engage.

How does handoff work?

▾
For Warm Leads, we provide contact details and inquiry context and make a warm introduction where appropriate. For Qualified Leads, we deliver the written Opportunity Brief and recommend a first-contact approach. For Co-Sell engagements, we stay involved through the close and transition out cleanly once the client-partner relationship is established.

How is confidentiality handled?

▾
All client information shared through any collaboration model is treated as confidential. Partners agree to use shared information solely for the purpose of evaluating and engaging the specific opportunity provided. Client details are not to be redistributed, shared with third parties, or used for any other purpose.

How quickly are leads delivered?

▾
Lead timing depends on our current pipeline and partner capacity. We don't guarantee volume or frequency — when an opportunity that matches a partner's profile comes in, we route it promptly. Qualified Leads take additional time to produce given the discovery process involved.

What happens if a client becomes a long-term retainer?

▾
For Co-Sell engagements, ongoing retainer arrangements and how compensation applies to them are addressed as part of the initial commercial discussion. We handle these on a case-by-case basis and document expectations clearly before any client introduction is made.
